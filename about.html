Our Story

Discover the passion and dedication behind AquaWind Kitesurfing

Born from Passion for the Ocean

AquaWind Kitesurfing was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate kitesurfing enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for this incredible sport with others. What started as weekend sessions with friends has grown into one of the most respected kitesurfing schools on the coast.

Our journey began when our founder, Sarah Mitchell, moved to the coast after years of traveling the world's best kitesurfing destinations. She realized that our local beach had perfect conditions but lacked proper instruction and safety standards.

Today, we've taught over 2,000 students, hosted international competitions, and built a community of riders who share our values of safety, respect for the ocean, and pure joy of flight.
